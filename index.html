thought offerings

by kevin moses

growing up I had two disjointed memories.

the first, was using a now-defunct website called The Thoughts Room to let go of negative thoughts or stressful emotions. no one saw what i typed, and each fading character lit up a star in the virtual night.

the second, was going to a guyanese hindu temple and experiencing agni prayers - rituals to feed a burning flame over hours. a prayer for a better life, cleansing of sin, or just someone (something) to listen to you.

i wanted to combine these memories into a interactive experience. are you stressed? anxious? type out your thoughts and watch them dissolve into the fire. the more thoughts you give, the longer it'll last.

no one will see or care what you write. hopefully you can use this as a safe space of sorts before going back out into the scary overstimulating internet or the real world.
